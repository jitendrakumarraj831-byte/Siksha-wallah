# **App Name**: Siksha Wallah Hub

## Core Features:

- Course Navigator: A categorized digital directory of all educational courses including B.Ed, Nursing, and Pharmacy for easy student browsing.
- AI Admission Advisor: An intelligent counselor tool that reasons through a student's past qualifications and future career goals to recommend the ideal educational pathway.
- One-Tap Connectivity: Floating direct-action buttons to instantly call Rajesh Sah or message via WhatsApp for immediate admission inquiries.
- Admission Inquiry Portal: A streamlined form for prospective students to submit their interest in specific courses directly to the administration.
- Local Search Optimizer: Geographical integration providing a precise map view and address details for the Forbesganj office near HP Petrol Pump.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Educational Blue (#3359ff), derived from a trustworthiness anchor, providing a professional and stable look for the brand.
- Background: A very light, desaturated blue wash (#f5f7ff) to maintain a clean, airy feel typical of high-end educational platforms.
- Accent: Vivid Azure (#33ccff), positioned 30 degrees from the primary hue to provide high contrast for call-to-action buttons.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' for tech-forward headlines and 'Inter' for clear, legible body text across the course listings.
- Minimalist line-art icons that reflect professional academic departments such as health, engineering, and management.
- A grid-based modular layout inspired by professional portfolio pieces, highlighting key services like 'B.Ed.' and 'MBBS' with emphasis.
- Smooth, purposeful transitions when expanding course details to guide the user's focus effortlessly.
